How to play
Catch stars
Each star is worth 10 pts. Rainbow stars give 50 pts. Catch in a row to build a combo multiplier up to x5.
Dodge clouds
Storm clouds drain a life and reset your combo. You have 3 lives. Lose them all and the game ends early.
Power-ups
Glowing orbs grant Shield (block hits), Magnet (pull stars), or Slow (halve fall speed) — each lasting 8 seconds.
Levels
Every 12 seconds the game advances a level. Falls get faster. Reach level 5 to become a star catcher master.
